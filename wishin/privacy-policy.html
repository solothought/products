Privacy Policy for WishIn

Effective Date: October 19, 2025
App Name: WishIn
Developer / Publisher: SoloThought
Homepage: https://solothought.com/products/WishIn/
Contact Email: solothought.official@gmail.com

1. Introduction

Thank you for using WishIn, a personal habit-building and reflection app developed by SoloThought. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data within the app.

By using WishIn, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We respect your privacy and limit data collection to what is necessary for improving the app and its features.

  • We do not collect personal identity information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • We do not access or read your contacts, messages, photos, or other files stored on your device.
  • We do not track your real-time location or activities outside this app.

Your personal entries (for example, your wishes, milestones, achievements, or purpose statements) and any attached media are stored locally on your device and remain accessible only to you.

Data Shared with Cloud Services

To help us understand app usage and enhance future versions, certain information may also be stored in Firebase Cloud, which is operated by Google. This may include:

  • Summaries of your activity (e.g., number of wishes, milestones, achievements)
  • Selected text or metadata that helps identify usage patterns or feature relevance
  • Technical and performance data (e.g., app version, timestamps, crash logs, device type)

This data is accessible to SoloThought solely for internal research, analysis, and app improvement purposes. It is not shared, sold, or used for advertising targeting beyond aggregated insights provided by Google Analytics and AdMob.

3. Local Storage and Backups

  • All your data (text, media, progress, etc.) is saved locally on your device only.
  • You may manually back up your data to a file on your device.
  • You can re-import the backup file into the app anytime.
  • More ways like Google Drive integration may be offered for backup purposes in future. If added, you will be informed and can choose whether or not to use it.

4. Analytics

The App uses cloud-based services to help understand usage patterns and improve features. Specifically, the App may transmit limited, structured data to Google Firebase and may use Google Analytics and related services. The kinds of information transmitted can include:

  • Counts and summaries of in-app activity (for example, number of entries, number of milestones or achievements added)
  • Selected metadata or short text snippets that help identify feature usage (for example, reason length, presence of media attachments)
  • Technical and performance data such as app version, device model, crash logs, timestamps, and network metadata

Purpose: This data is used solely by SoloThought for internal analysis, product improvement, and to monitor app reliability and performance. It is not sold to third parties.

Access: Data stored in Firebase may be accessed by authorized SoloThought personnel for research and analysis. Aggregated or anonymized reports may also be provided by Google Analytics.

Third-party policies: These services are provided by Google and are subject to Google’s terms and privacy policies. Learn more:

  • Google Analytics Terms
  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Advertisements

The app displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain data to personalize ads and improve ad performance. This is governed by Google’s privacy policy.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

6. Children’s Privacy

WishIn is suitable for all age groups. We do not knowingly collect or request any personal information from children. All data remains on the user’s device.

7. Permissions Used

WishIn may request the following permissions only when required:

  • Storage access: to save and import backup files created by the user.

The app does not access any other system data or personal information.

8. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services in future versions:

  • Google AdMob (for ads)
  • Google Analytics (for app performance insights)

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy, which you can review below:

  • Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Google Analytics: https://policies.google.com/privacy
